# Engineering Judgment Newsletter Strategy

## Objective

Transition from publishing standalone LinkedIn articles to building a
recognizable, long-term newsletter brand centered around **Engineering
Judgment**.

The goal is to create a consistent publication that showcases expertise
in municipal infrastructure, engineering judgment, and infrastructure
analytics while gradually building a subscriber base.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Why a Newsletter Instead of Standalone Articles?

A LinkedIn newsletter provides several advantages over individual
articles:

- Subscribers are notified whenever a new edition is published.
- Each edition contributes to a growing publication instead of
    existing as an isolated article.
- Readers begin following the publication rather than discovering
    articles individually.
- The newsletter establishes a stronger professional brand over time.

Rather than being known as someone who occasionally writes articles, the
objective is to become the publisher of a respected technical
newsletter.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Recommended Launch Strategy

## Issue #001 -- Welcome Edition

Rather than immediately republishing an existing article, publish a
short welcome edition introducing the newsletter.

Suggested content:

- Why the newsletter exists
- Who it is written for
- What readers can expect
- Topics that will be covered

Potential closing:

> Future issues will explore infrastructure reliability, hydraulic
> engineering, asset management, predictive analytics, engineering
> software, and lessons learned from more than 25 years in municipal
> engineering.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Republish Existing Articles

Existing articles should **not** simply be copied and pasted.

Instead:

- Update the content.
- Improve the introduction.
- Add additional insights where appropriate.
- Treat each as a revised or second edition.

This allows existing work to become part of the permanent newsletter
archive while still providing value to readers.

## Number Every Edition

Include an issue number with each publication.

Example:

_**Engineering Judgment**

_**Issue #002**

*Utilities Don't Have a Data Problem. They Have a Decision Problem.*

Numbering reinforces that readers are following an ongoing technical
publication rather than a collection of unrelated articles.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Long-Term Vision

Over time, the newsletter should become a professional resource focused
on the intersection of:

- Engineering judgment
- Operational data
- Predictive analytics
- Infrastructure reliability
- Asset management
- Hydraulic engineering
- Capital planning

Rather than simply writing articles, the objective is to build a
respected publication that documents ideas, lessons learned, and
practical approaches to improving infrastructure decision-making.
